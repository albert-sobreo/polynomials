ENRICHMENT

  1. ) w\(^2\) + 10w = 11

    2.   steps:
        A.
              a = 5
              a\(^2\) = 25
        B.
              w\(^2\) + 10w + 25 = 36
              √(w + 5)\(^2\) = √36
              w + 5 = ±6
        C.   first solution:
              w + 5 = 6
              w = 6 - 5
              w = 1
        D.   second solution:
              w + 5 = - 6
              w = - 6 - 5
              w = - 11

  2. ) g\(^2\) + 7g - (51/4) = 0

    3.   steps:
        A.
              a = 7/2
              a\(^2\) = 49/4
        B.
              g\(^2\) + 7g - (49/4) = 100/4
              √(g + (7/2))\(^2\) = √(100/4)
             
        C.   first solution:
              g + (7/2) = 10/2
              g = (10/2) - (7/2)
              g = 3/2
        D.   second solution:
              g + (7/2) = -(10/2)
              g = -(10/2) - (7/2)
              g = - (17/2)
